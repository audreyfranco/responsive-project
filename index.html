Lalaland Kind Cafe

Lalaland Kind Cafe is a coffee shop that is located at The Grove. It sells very delicious coffees, pastries, and breakfast items.

Address:

189 The Grove Drive Suite G. 1C Building S10, Los Angeles, CA 90036

What I like about it

Whenever I go to the Grove and the farmer's market, I like to stop by and get a coffee and one of their toasts. I try something new every time. I especially love getting a coffee and going to the bookstore to browse and shop for books.

Grand Central Market

Grand Central Market is a food hall in Downtown Los Angeles. They have different vendors and options for you to choose from.

Address:

317 S Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90013

What I like about it

I like that you have different options with what you would want to eat or drink. If you go with a group of friends each person can get whichever option they would like.
